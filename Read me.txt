# 📊 Sentiment Analysis on Child Welfare Tweets (May–June 2009)

This project analyzes over 1.6 million tweets to understand public sentiment around child-related themes like education, health, child rights, and more.

## 🔍 Objectives

- Analyze how people feel about child welfare topics on Twitter.
- Explore trends across different themes (Education, Health, Abuse, etc.).
- Understand the emotional impact of real-world events (e.g., Pakistan crisis, Iran protests).

## 📂 Project Structure

- `data/` – Raw and processed datasets.
- `visuals/` – Graphs and word clouds used in the report.
- `report/` – Final project report (PDF/DOCX).
- `code/` – Python scripts for cleaning, processing ,analysing and visualizing
